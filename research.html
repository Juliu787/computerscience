Research

Research at Department of Computer Science

Software engineering, computer networking, data communication, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and optimization are the current research topics being worked on in the department. Additionally, studies are being conducted in the field of computer-aided learning, with a focus on the application of computers to teaching in the country's primary and secondary education systems. The Department is also investigating the use of robots to assist children with autism in their day-to-day activities. The teaching staff has published far more now than before.
